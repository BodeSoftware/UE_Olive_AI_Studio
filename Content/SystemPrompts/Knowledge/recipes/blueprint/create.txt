CREATE new Blueprint:
1. blueprint.create -> {path:"/Game/Blueprints/BP_Name", parent_class:"Actor"}
2. add_component + add_variable (batch all in one turn)
3. blueprint.apply_plan_json (ALL graph logic in ONE call)

Do NOT call blueprint.read before create -- the asset does not exist yet.
Do NOT call preview_plan_json in the same turn as apply.
Data-provider steps (get_var, pure calls) MUST appear BEFORE steps that @ref them.
All graph nodes go in one plan_json call -- never add_node one-at-a-time for 3+ nodes.
